Pomeranian

Pomeranians are closely related to wolves. They’re a spitz breed, meaning a type of dog that has several wolf-like characteristics.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Weight: 1.9-3.5 kilograms
  • Average Lifespan: 12-16 years
  • Origin: Germany, Poland

Pomeranians are small dogs weighing 1.9–3.5 kilograms (4.2–7.7 lb) and standing 5.0–11 inches (13–28 cm) high at the withers. They are compact but sturdy dogs with an abundant textured coat with a highly plumed tail set high and flat.
